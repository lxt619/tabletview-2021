About

Hi, I am a graduate student in the UX track in MSI of Umich who is on the way to becoming a UX/UI designer. I got my bachelor's degree with honors at Hofstra University where I had many opportunities to learn and practice multi-disciplinary projects.


I'd like to become a designer who is problem-solving and creating design concepts/experiences that empower people. I focus on using technologies to solve the problem of emotional communication between people.
